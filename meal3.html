Crock Pot Santa Fe Chicken

Quick prep, shredded chicken

Ingredients

Amount Ingredient
1 and 1/2 lb Chicken breast
14.4 oz Can diced tomatoes with chilis
15 oz Can black beans
8 oz Frozen corn
14.4 oz Can fat free chicken broth
1 tsp Garlic powder
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Cayenne pepper
Dash Salt

Instructions

Step Instruction
1 Combine chicken broth, beans (drained), corn, tomatoes, cilantro, scallions, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, cayenne pepper and salt in theÂ crock pot
2 Season chicken breast with salt and lay on top
3 Cook on high for 4 to 6 hours
4 30 min. before serving, remove chicken and shred
5 Return chicken to slow cooker and stir in. Adjust salt and seasoning to taste
6 Serve over rice or tortillas and with your favorite toppings
