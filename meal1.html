Baked Chicken Parm

Italian classic, ligtened up

Ingredients

Amount Ingredient
4 whole Chicken breasts
3/4 cup Seasoned breadcrumbs
1/4 cup Grated parm cheese
2 tbsp Olive oil
3/4 cup Mozz cheese (reduced fat)
1 cup Marinara
Dash Cooking spray

Instructions

Step Instruction
1 Preheat oven to 450°F. Spray a large baking sheet lightly
2 Combine breadcrumbs and parm cheese in a bowl. Melt the butter in another bowl. Lightly brush the butter onto the chicken, then dip into breadcrumb mixture.  Place on baking sheet and repeat
3 Lightly spray a oil on top and bake in the oven for 25 minutes
4 Remove from oven, spoon 1 tbsp sauce over each piece of chicken and top each with 1 1/2 tbsp of shredded mozz cheese
5 Bake 5 more minutes or until cheese is melted
